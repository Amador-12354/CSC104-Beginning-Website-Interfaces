Home

Minecraft is a game of blocks, creatures, and community. In this game there are different modes you can play from surviving the night to just building a work of art. Minecraft was created back in 2009 that was published by Mojang Studios.

A list of the Mobs to look out for:

Creeper
This mob is commonly seen and likes to seek up on the player and explode
Zombie
This mob is seen during the night which deals melee damage, by coming up to you and hitting you. They can attack in groups
Skeleton
Can be seen during the night like the zombie. They shoot arrows so they are diffcult to attack and slay
Witch
Can be seen sometimes during the night but can be found in the swamp biome. The withc uses potions in combat can be splash potions (hurts players) and drinkable potions (to heal themselves)

Some Popular Blocks:

Dirt
This block is everywhere. You can get by just punching it or using a shovel.
Cobblestone
To get this block you need a pickaxe and just have to mine stone found in mountains and caves.
Logs
These are just trees so you need to a axe or just your fist to punch the tree to get the logs. You can use the logs to make planks. (MOST IMPORTANT BLOCK - in my opinion)

How to Survive Your First Night

Here is a short walkthrough of how to survive your first night.

Step One:
Punch any trees to make planks
Step Two:
Make a crafting table by placing four planks in the crafting area found in your inventory.
Step Three:
Build a shelter either of wood planks or dirt
Step Four:
Make tools the most important is the sword and pickaxe
